What is Cloudbrain?

Cloudbrain is a free, open source platform for bio-sensor data analytics.

Our goal is to promote open science and to empower you to keep ocntrol of your own biodata. We want to make it easy for you to store, visualize, and analyze data.

On.Your.Own.Terms.

Features

Send Data

Connect your device and start streaming.

Store Data

Save your sessions and have them accessible from anywhere.

Analyze Data

Digital Signal Processing and Machine Learning Modules.

Visualize Data

Different ways to look at your recorded and live data.
